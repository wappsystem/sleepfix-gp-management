Screening Questions

We invite you to complete the below questions to determine if you are eligible for the study. These questions should take approximately 2 minutes to complete. Once you complete all the questions, click the ‘Next’ button at the bottom of the page.

1. Do you live in Australia?

2. Are you aged between 18-65?

3. Are you fluent in English?

4. Do you have a smartphone?

5. Are you planning to have a baby or currently pregnant?

6. Are you a shift worker (i.e. work commencing before 6am or later than 8pm)?

7. Have you been diagnosed with any of the following physical health conditions: cardiovascular disease (stroke, heart failure), neurological disorders (epilepsy, motor neuron disease, Parkinson’s disease)*

8. Have you been diagnosed with any of the following mental health conditions: schizophrenia, psychosis, mania*

9. Have you travelled to a destination with a time difference of more than 2 hours in the past month?

10. Do you anticipate on traveling to a destination more than an hour time difference in the next 12 weeks?

11. Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by thoughts that you would be better off dead, or of hurting yourself





*If unsure, please contact the research team.
